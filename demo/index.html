NomNomDom

Hello, welcome to Nom-Nom-Dom.

Nom-Nom-Dom is a DOM manipulation program that allows its users to do cool things like adding and removing DOM's!

Try adding and completing a to-do!

To-Do List

***Click "Add" to submit***

Adding a to-do is a combination of finding, making and appending a DOM.

Removing a to-do is a combination of finding its parent DOM, removing and emptying a DOM.

Nom-Nom-Dom can add, remove, make AJAX calls and more!
